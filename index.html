Singular location for broad applications.

A site for a variety of exigencies developed with speed, security, and ease of use in mind. Whether you need to utilize a general application or play a game that helps cultivate a skill this website has it all. New apps or games are being added/developed but we are open to suggesstions.

Our Principles

Storage

Companies can use data for positive things, such as sending a happy birthday email to a user. However, companies can also use such data for selfish and manipulative purposes. As an illustration, selling said personal information to third parties.

We at Alione do not believe in such selfish acts; therefore, we do not store any personal identifying information that includes, but is not limited to, emails, passwords, or birthdays.

Speed

After clicking on a link, do you wait a few seconds for the designated new page to load? If so, you have experienced a late 20th-century paradigm implemented in a 21st-century timeline.

For this reason, once one instance of Alione has loaded, the exigency for subsequent loads is eradicated, a ramification of utilizing the single web application paradigm.

Security

It is not an aberration to read online about companies infringing on the security of their users through selfish acts; or companies putting their users in a situation where their safety can be violated.

Therefore before any decision is made -- whether that be trivial or imperative --, we premise each decision on the impact on our users. If a decision were to impact our users negatively, such a decision would not be employed or executed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the point of the games?

Each game contains a purpose to cultivate a skill while maintaining a fun environment. As an illustration, the mind game is premised on sharpening your short-term memory through a see-encode-retrieval process. In addition, the typing game cultivates one's ability to type faster with a variety of texts.

I have an idea for an application to put on this website; how may I contribute?

There are two ways one can contribute:

  1. Email an idea to us directly at mario.molinito7@gmail.com
  2. Start a pull request with code you implemented to a specific section of the website at github.com/molinitomario/alione
Is there a way to create an account?

Unfortunately, we do not provide the option to create an account; as one of our principles -- security -- we decided that creating an account merely increases a user's risk to threats.

Why is my history cleared after closing all tabs relating to this website?

We utilize session storage which functions as a box we store your history in during the remainder of your stay while on this website. Once a user leaves the website, their respective box is discarded. However, we provide an option to enable local storage, which elongates the box's existence -- in fact, the box is not discarded unless done manually through your browser options.
